My Campus Life

My journey at UTHM has been a transformative experience, filled with opportunities for personal and professional growth. One of the most memorable highlights was participating in the UTHM Hackathon, where my team and I embarked on an exciting challenge to build a mobile app prototype within a limited timeframe. This high-pressure environment taught me the importance of teamwork, creativity, and effective time management as we collaborated intensely to bring our idea to fruition. The experience not only sharpened my coding skills but also bolstered my confidence, proving that I could contribute meaningfully to innovative projects.

In addition to the hackathon, I had the privilege of joining a Laravel bootcamp with my instructor, which significantly enhanced my understanding of web development. This intensive course provided a hands-on experience, allowing me to dive deep into the Laravel framework and explore its powerful capabilities. Learning alongside passionate peers in a supportive environment inspired me to push my boundaries and strive for excellence. Although I didn't participate in an industrial visit to Intel in Penang, I sought other practical learning experiences that enriched my knowledge of the tech industry, such as attending workshops and networking events.

Volunteering at blood donation drives organized by the university was another rewarding aspect of my campus life. Contributing to a cause that directly impacts the community reinforced my belief in the importance of giving back and made me appreciate the collective effort involved in such initiatives. Additionally, I cherished the moments spent with friends during our trip to Langkawi over the semester break. This adventure was a perfect blend of relaxation and exploration, creating lasting memories that I will carry with me long after graduation. Overall, my campus life has been a vibrant tapestry of learning, growth, and meaningful connections that continue to shape my journey.
